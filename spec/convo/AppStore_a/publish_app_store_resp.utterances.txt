publish_app_store_resp

If you want to publish new apps or new versions, check the requirements below:
**1.** Confirmation that there is a PIA request in place (in review) inside OneTrust;
**2.** Confirmation on the ABI owner of the app;
**3.** Confirmation on the list of access for devs and app managers;
**4.** Check if PIA and Apple policy privacy (if app is for Apple store) are matching;
**5.** If everything is ok, development team will be able to proceed and publish the app.
